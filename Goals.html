Mission Statement

Our Commitment

"Sehal Welfare Foundation is dedicated to transforming the lives of individuals and families living in poverty, by providing access to education, economic opportunities, and resources, enabling them to break the cycle of poverty and improve their overall well-being."

Sehal Welfare Foundation provides access to Montessori to advanced IT training, vocational training, freelance work, and skills development programs. We offer economic opportunities and entrepreneurship support, special education, therapies, and social support services for special needs children. Our approach fosters community engagement, social cohesion, and collective empowerment.

We show empathy and understanding towards individuals in poverty, enabling them to take control of their lives. We serve all individuals regardless of background, race, or religion and operate with transparency, accountability, and ethics.

Objectives

Our Goals

"Sehal Welfare Foundation is here to empower families of special children by providing accessible and affordable therapies, guidance, and support, enabling them to reach their full potential and live fulfilling lives, while fostering a community of inclusivity, understanding, and compassion."

  • Provide subsidized or free therapies (e.g., ABA therapy, occupational therapy, speech therapy) for children with conditions such as Autism, ADHD, Down syndrome, and more.
  • Offer guidance and training for parents on managing their child's condition.
  • Create a support network for families to connect and share experiences.
  • Raise awareness and promote inclusivity in the community.
  • Collaborate with healthcare professionals and organizations to amplify impact.
  • Continuously evaluate and improve services to meet evolving needs.
