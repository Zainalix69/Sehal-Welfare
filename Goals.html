Mission Statement

Our Commitment

"Sehal Welfare Foundation is dedicated to transforming the lives of individuals and families living in poverty, by providing access to education, economic opportunities, and resources, enabling them to break the cycle of poverty and improve their overall well-being."

Sehal Welfare Foundation provides access to Montessori to advanced IT training, vocational training, freelance work, and skills development programs. We offer economic opportunities and entrepreneurship support, special education, therapies, and social support services for special needs children. Our approach fosters community engagement, social cohesion, and collective empowerment.

We show empathy and understanding towards individuals in poverty, enabling them to take control of their lives. We serve all individuals regardless of background, race, or religion and operate with transparency, accountability, and ethics.

Objectives

Our Goals

"Sehal Welfare Foundation is here to empower families of special children by providing accessible and affordable therapies, guidance, and support, enabling them to reach their full potential and live fulfilling lives, while fostering a community of inclusivity, understanding, and compassion."

  • Education for All: Provide comprehensive education from Montessori to advanced levels, integrating with modern emerging digital tools.
  • Technical Training: Establish training centers equipped with modern technology to offer free courses in e-commerce, freelancing, and other IT-related fields to foster self-reliance and employment.
  • Healthcare for All: Establish healthcare centers providing essential medicines, pregnancy and other medical treatments, and medical tests at subsidized rates to ensure access for everyone, including humans and animals. We work to ease the financial burden on low-income families.
  • Special Needs Support: Develop a rehabilitation center for special children, offering tailored education and therapeutic services to ensure their inclusion and development.
  • Women Empowerment and Vocational Training: Create vocational training programs like nursing training to provide women with rewarding careers in healthcare. The programs also include skill development in sewing, embroidery, beautician services, cooking, computer literacy, and more. We aim to empower uneducated, less educated, and highly educated women to support themselves and their families.
  • Collective Weddings and Support: Provide bridal attire for brides and grooms, and distribute food and dowry items to deserving and impoverished individuals. Organize collective weddings to facilitate marriages in a simple and respectful manner.
  • Green and Affordable Energy Solutions: Implement solar energy solutions for households and community centers at subsidized rates to reduce electricity costs and promote sustainability.
