Our Projects

The Projects we care about

1. *Education for All*: Providing general and technical education to children and adults, including special education for children with special needs.

2. *Skill Development*: Offering training in IT, e-commerce, freelancing, and other technical fields to reduce unemployment and promote self-reliance.

3. *Women's Empowerment*: Supporting vocational training and job creation efforts for women, regardless of their education level.

4. *Rehabilitation*: Establishing centers for the rehabilitation of special children.

5. *Madrasah Students*: Providing IT and technical training to students of Madaras to help them earn a livelihood with confidence.

6. *Youth Training*: Running institutions like Sehal School of Youth Training to prepare the younger generation for the future.

7. *Inclusive Syllabus Development*: Creating a comprehensive syllabus from Montessori to 8th grade that includes Quranic education, conversational skills in multiple languages, and practical computer education.

These initiatives reflect the foundation's commitment to empowering individuals and communities through education, training, and support.
