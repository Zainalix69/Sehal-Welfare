President Statement
Captain (Retired) Ch Khalil Ahmed

I am an educationist with a Master's in Education, has been in the field of education since 2002. As the President of Sehal Welfare Foundation, I will try to bring a wealth of experience and a deep commitment to uplifting underserved communities. I have been at the helm of EFA Montessori and High School and EFA College, institutions registered with government authorities and affiliated with the Federal Board of Education Islamabad. Under my leadership, these institutions have been providing quality education to low-profile areas, making significant strides in improving educational outcomes for underserved populations.

My vision and dedication continue to drive Sehal Welfare Foundation's mission of empowering individuals especially woman through education, IT training and vocational training, fostering self-reliance, and promoting sustainable community development. My vision is to create a world where every special child can reach their full potential, thrive and feel valued. I strive to make a significant contribution in the betterment of the society by accomplishing Sehal Welfare Foundation goals.

Remember every small action counts, and collective efforts can lead to significant positive change.

Sehal Welfare Foundation is dedicated to transforming lives through education, vocational training, and self-reliance initiatives. We strive to empower individuals by providing comprehensive education from Montessori to advanced IT training, supporting technical education in e-commerce and freelance work, and offering specialized support for special needs children. Our goal is to overcome unemployment, uplift communities, and support to the youth of Pakistan.

Our Mission

Our vision is to create a society where every individual has the opportunity to achieve their fullest potential through access to quality education, technical and vocational training. We aspire to build a community that is self-reliant, confident, and capable of overcoming economic challenges. By fostering inclusivity, supporting special needs education, and promoting technical skills, we aim to uplift and empower the underserved, ensuring sustainable development and prosperity for all
