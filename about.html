About us

An international independent Charity organization

Your support will help us improve the lives of vulnerable and underprivileged children, ensuring they have a better future and opportunities for a brighter tomorrow.

Sehal Welfare Foundation is dedicated to transforming lives through a holistic approach to education and vocational training. Our commitment lies in empowering individuals from all walks of life, equipping them with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed and contribute positively to society.

Sehal Welfare Foundation is dedicated to transforming lives through education, vocational training, and self-reliance initiatives. We strive to empower individuals by providing comprehensive education from Montessori to advanced IT training, supporting technical education in e-commerce and freelance work, and offering specialized support for special needs children. Our goal is to overcome unemployment, uplift communities, and support to the youth of Pakistan.

Vision of Sehal Welfare Foundation is empowering individuals and communities to thrive in a rapidly changing world, by providing access to cutting-edge education, skill development, unlock their potential and freelancing opportunities, fostering self-belief, independence, and social inclusion, thereby creating a self sufficient society and a better world .Transforming lives of underprivileged individuals by equipping women and individuals with diverse skills, knowledge and confidence. We aim to provide comprehensive support, resources and advocacy to special children and their families fostering a society that embraces inclusivity, understanding and compassion ,thereby breaking the cycle of poverty and fostering a more inclusive and equitable society.

1. Education for All: Provide comprehensive education from Montessori to advanced levels, integrating with modern emerging digital tools.

2. *Vocational and Technical Training:* Establish training centers equipped with modern technology to offer free courses in e-commerce, freelancing, and other IT-related fields to foster self-reliance and employment opportunities.

3. *Special Needs Support:* Develop a rehabilitation center for special children, offering tailored education and therapeutic services to ensure their inclusion and development.

4. *Women Empowerment:* Create vocational training programs for uneducated, less educated, and highly educated women to enable them to support themselves and their families.

5. *Economic Development:* Promote entrepreneurship and job creation through technical and IT support training for students of Madaras, enabling them to earn a livelihood confidently.

6. *Community Upliftment:* Foster a supportive community environment that encourages continuous learning, skill development, and personal growth.

7. *Sustainable Development:* Implement initiatives that ensure long-term economic stability and growth for individuals and communities
